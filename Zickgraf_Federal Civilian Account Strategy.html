The Strategic Frame

AITGS pursues four federal civilian accounts where our Microsoft platform capabilities, ANC 8(a) sole-source authority, and branded solution portfolio create compounding competitive advantages. ArcticPass is a door-opener and differentiator — not a ceiling. The goal at each agency is to land on a permitting or modernization program, then expand into the broader Microsoft modernization relationship. Our 8(a) ANC status removes the dollar cap on sole-source awards, making us the path of least resistance for COs who want to move fast with a qualified, mission-aligned partner.
